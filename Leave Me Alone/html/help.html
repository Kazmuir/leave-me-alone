How does the extension work


You want to clean your invitation requests

1/Click on "invitation manager page" in the extension or go directly to this URL: https://www.linkedin.com/mynetwork/invitation-manager/

2/The LMA extension will delete all invitations it finds with the list of keywords you've picked. If I want to delete all the invitations received from recruiters I will choose the keywords "recruiter", "hunter", "consultant". To choose the keywords, click on the settings buttons and add your own keywords if you wish.

3/Go back to the linkedin invitation page and click on the "Leave Me Alone" button of the extension, it'll take a few moments if you have many invitations pending.


You want to clean your existing connections

1/Go to your connections page located here: https://www.linkedin.com/mynetwork/invite-connect/connections/

2/The LMA extension will delete all connections it finds with the list of keywords you've picked. If I want to delete all the invitations received from recruiters I will choose the keywords "recruiter", "hunter", "consultant". To choose the keywords, click on the settings buttons and add your own keywords if you wish.

3/Go back to the linkedin connections page and click on the "Leave Me Alone(existing)" button of the extension, it'll take a few moments if you have many connections.

4/At then end you'll see a popup to confirm if you really want to delete these connections.
